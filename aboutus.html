Robert "Preacher" Humphries ... Co-founder

Rob has been in the paranormal research business since 1997. He co-founded Total Paranormal Investigations (TPI) as well as Carolina Paranormal Rescue (CPR). On August 2nd, 2017, he and Brian merged CPR with Ghost Commander to create an elite team called Pee Dee Region Paranormal Group (PDRPG).

Rob is also credited with inventing some of the tools used by PDRPG during investigations, and he likes to modify other tools to make them more trustworthy. He has experienced a lot throughout his life that he still can't explain and continues to seek answers.

Brian "Reaper" Horton ... Co-founder

Hey, I am Brian (Reaper) Horton and I am a co-founder of PDRPG. I have been interested in the paranormal field since the age of 13. As I was growing up, I found myself always getting books on the paranormal. I am also a proud veteran. I served in the US Navy as a machinist mate, and in the Army National Guard as a combat engineer.

I started in 2005 as an investigator with Sandhills Paranormal Investigations of North Carolina. I thank them for adding me. From there I joined East Koast Paranormal. It was a blast. I got to meet a lot of good people. Time went by and I decided to start my own team with Pops (Rick) called Ghost Commander Paranormal. I have investigated all over the East and West Coast and met a lot of great teams. I also worked with a production crew on a trail for a paranormal show. All my experiences in the paranormal had been great.

On to the new team: One day my wife was at a friend's house and, low and behold, Rob was there. My wife asked him if he still did the paranormal stuff, he told her yes and said we should contact each other. We've known each other since day camp days. So I called Rob up and we decided to merge groups to form the elite team Pee Dee Region Paranormal Group. Best move ever. When we take you on as a client we feel after that you are family. I love this field and helping those that need answers. It's great that my loving wife supports my team and it makes this passion more fun.

Scott "Phantom" Tomestic ... Lead Investigator / Webmaster

Scott is a lead investigator for PDRP and the group's webmaster. He is also the only team member not originally from the Pee Dee Region, having moved to North Carolina in summer 2016 from his home state of Massachusetts.

Scott has been fascinated by the paranormal for most of his life and fueled his interest by studying books, watching TV shows and attending lectures on the subject. He has also visited many spooky hotspots in notorious locations like Salem, MA, Gettysburg, PA and Savannah, GA.

When he's not investigating the paranormal, Scott works from home as a clinical data specialist and also maintains a paranormal blog. He lives in Rockingham's historic district with his wife, 5 cats and 2 rabbits.
